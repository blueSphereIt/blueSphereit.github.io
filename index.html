Solutions




Face Recognition


We offer robust, scalable and accurate facial recognition for 1:1 verifications. Our facial recognition is specifically trained to distinguish Bangladeshi facial features. Our system works on most devices and does not require special cameras.



Facial Identification


Our facial identification system is made for identifying people in crowds and for public surveillance for 1:n identifications. This helps our client monitor their facilities and maintain logs and analytics of who has been in their facilities.



Custom Object Recognition


We offer highly accurate AI object detection and analytics in a user friendly package where we can custom train objects for the user's use case. Our system can not only detect objects, but track and understand their interactions.



Scene Recognition


Our scene recognition technology uses three layers of AI to understand what is happening in a video feed and let the user know in real time. This helps our clients save thousands of hours in monitoring costs.
